Teacher → Student Workflow

①
Teacher opens the Recording Studio and records pādas for a Varga.
②
Teacher exports a Varga ZIP (all recorded pādas bundled as WAV files).
③
Teacher shares the ZIP file with students by email, WhatsApp, or any file-sharing method.
④
Students open the Practice App, tap + Import in the Audio section, and import the ZIP as a named Audio Set.
⑤
Students select the teacher's Audio Set as their audio source and practise with the teacher's voice.
